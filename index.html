Lindt Truffles Collector

Instructions:

You will be given a random number at the start of the game

There are four Lindt truffles below. By clicking on a crystal you will add a specific ammount of points to your total score.

You win the game by matching your total score to the random number, you lose the game if your total score goes above the random number

The value of each truffle is hidden from you until you click on it.

Each time the game starts the values of each truffle will change
